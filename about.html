About Kazakhstan

Geography

Spanning over 2.7 million square kilometers, Kazakhstan is the world's largest landlocked country and the ninth-largest nation globally. Our diverse terrain encompasses flatlands, steppes, taigas, canyons, hills, deltas, mountains, and deserts, offering a myriad of landscapes to explore.

Languages

Kazakh, our official language, resonates in the hearts of our people, while Russian is widely spoken, especially in business and daily interactions, reflecting our nation's diverse cultural tapestry.

Culture

Rooted in nomadic traditions and enriched by various ethnic influences, Kazakhstan's culture is a vibrant celebration of festivals, music, dance, and art, each telling a story of our enduring heritage.

Leisure Activities

Adventure awaits in Kazakhstan. Hike the majestic Tien Shan mountains, traverse the vast steppes, or experience the warmth of Kazakh hospitality in traditional yurt stays. Our diverse landscapes and rich traditions offer countless opportunities for unforgettable experiences.
